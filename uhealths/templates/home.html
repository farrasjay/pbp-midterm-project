What is uHealths?

uHealths is a simple health related application that is currently web based. uHealths provide various information and features that helps users gain more insights about their general health.

Motivation

Our topic drives all the way back to one of G20's topic, which is Global Health, that strives to educate people on global health and its effects on the world. Our idea thrives in an online health and wellness website that offers insight into the most current health and wellness, as well as contents that helps our user to be able to live healthier lives.

Story Behind

uHealths is a project made by 5 Faculty of Computer Science UI students. This project reflects one of G20's topics, that is Global Health. We hope uHealths is of great use to you!
